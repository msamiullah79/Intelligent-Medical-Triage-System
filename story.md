# DS Universe Story

In the bustling world of Medica Universe, emergencies appeared like sudden storms.
Heap Sami stood tall, always placing the most critical patient at the very top.
BST Talha guarded the grand library of patient records, sorted with perfect balance.
HashTable Hadi zipped across the land, instantly retrieving any patient’s information.
LinkedList Rahman connected every patient’s history from the first visit to the last.
Together, they formed the mighty Triage Guardians, protectors of medical order.
One day, a mysterious wave of illness flooded the triage halls with new patients.
The wise Logistic Oracle predicted the severity of each case with mathematical precision.
With their combined powers, the Guardians organized, sorted, searched, and saved countless lives.
Thus, the Intelligent Triage System kept Medica Universe safe, efficient, and ever vigilant.
